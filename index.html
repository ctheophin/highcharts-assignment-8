Life Expectancy in the Caribbean

By Courtney Theophin

We take a look at the life expectancy for certain Caribbean countries, using the data from the World Bank. These statistics are a combined life expectancy both men and women in 2016. Cuba has the highest life expectancy out of all the countries shown, with 79.742 years of age. Haiti has the lowest with a rate of 63.33 years.
